About GTX Hosty

Established in 2022, Gtxhosty was founded with the goal of creating a dependable domain provider, cloud hosting platform and providing the highest level of customer satisfaction in the industry - and that is our commitment. We do not accept anything less and are dedicated to maintaining our presence in the industry. Our team of experts has accumulated over two years of experience exclusively in the hosting sector. We are reputable and enthusiastic, with a strong foundation. We take pride in being one of the few privately-owned, independent global web hosting companies in the industry. This means that we have full ownership of our business, enabling us to prioritize our customers and continuously enhance our cloud hosting platform.

Our History

GTX Hosty: The Cloud Hosting Platform that Grew from College

Founded by college students in July 2022, GTX Hosty began as a web hosting service for students and has since expanded to provide cloud hosting for businesses. Our focus is on delivering top-notch customer satisfaction, reliability, security, and efficiency.

Rapid Customer Growth

In December 2022, GTX Hosty reached a milestone by acquiring our first 100 customers. Our dedication to top-notch cloud hosting services and exceptional customer support has led to over 1,000 satisfied clients in just a few months.

.com Domain Registrar

In August 2023, GTX Hosty was recognized as a cost-effective and reliable registrar for .com domains. Their commitment to providing affordable and high-quality domain registration services has earned them this recognition in the industry. With their competitive pricing and excellent customer support, GTX Hosty has become a trusted choice for individuals and businesses looking to secure their online presence with a .com domain.

Added load balancer and Auto scaling

In December 2023, we added new features to improve system performance and scalability, including a load balancer to distribute network traffic and auto-scaling to adjust capacity based on demand. These enhancements have significantly improved system reliability and efficiency for a better user experience.
